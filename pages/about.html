About Me

Hello everyone! This is Sagnik Singha here, the guy behind "lightworks" and this website. From my childhood I have been fascinated by technology and photography.

My obsession with photography started in 2015 when I first got a Nikon Coolpix S6300, a point and shoot for taking pictures of the trips I went to. However, I really got into photography when I got my DSLR, the Canon EOS 1500D in 2023. I got very serious about photography, and always looked for ways to improve it. The quality difference is really visible between my Arunachal Pradesh trip and my Kashmir trip. Hopefully I will be able to continues this my whole life.

I love taking landscape photos than anything else. I also love astrophotography and love processing astrophotographs. Candid pictures of people are nice too.

Being very invested into technology I started my college this year, pursuing a B.Tech course in Computer Science and Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. I am now in the photography and space club of our college and I hope to further hone my skills there.

Thank you for reading!
